The Way Forward

After a lot of thought about what my next step is going to be, I decided that I wanted to re-invent myself. I have always been interested in technology, software, implementations of solutions and efficiency. I remember I had my first email address back in 1995, I I could only check it while going to the specialized UNIX computer center of my university. I was amazed about speed of conversations and message delivery. At that time Altavista, Excite and Yahoo where the dominant force in web search. Now things have changed so rapidly and are in a continuous development. My son now is used to having any inquiry resolved by asking it to the phone.

I'm really excited about what I'm going to learn at Galvanize. We will be able to not only understand what makes the web work, but also be a part of the development and growth of technology. At the end of the day, what we are doing is only going to help us grow as a society, with better solutions in our hands and better communication.

I hope what I learn in Galvanize is the start of a fruitful career. Knowledge is not enough to succeed, though. You also have to have a good network that is supportive and help you progress.

One year ago I had no idea this was going to be my path; life has many twists and turns but I'm lucky to have found this one. I wish all my classmates the best and look forward to having the best learning experience!
